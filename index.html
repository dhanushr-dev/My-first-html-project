Salaar is a high-octane action thriller that follows the story of Deva, a quiet yet exceptionally powerful man whose past is filled with mystery. His life changes when circumstances force him to honor an old friendship, drawing him into a violent struggle for power in the kingdom of Khansaar. As rival factions battle for control, Deva must confront dangerous enemies, hidden secrets, and difficult choices that test his loyalty and courage. Directed by Prashanth Neel, the film is known for its spectacular action choreography, large-scale production, breathtaking visuals, and emotionally driven narrative. Supported by a talented cast and an intense musical score, Salaar blends friendship, betrayal, politics, and action into an engaging cinematic experience. The movie offers thrilling sequences, memorable characters, and a gripping storyline that keeps audiences invested from beginning to end.

KGF Chapter 2 - 124.7 million & BOX PFFICE COLLECTION: ₹1200+ crore 😯

KGF Chapter 2 is a blockbuster action drama that continues the epic story of Rocky after his rise to power in the Kolar Gold Fields (KGF). Determined to protect his empire and fulfill the promise he made to his mother, Rocky faces new enemies, political opposition, and powerful rivals who threaten everything he has built. As his influence grows, he becomes one of the most feared and respected figures in the country. The film features breathtaking action sequences, emotional storytelling, stunning cinematography, and a powerful background score that enhances every scene. Directed by Prashanth Neel, the movie combines larger-than-life visuals with intense performances, making it one of the biggest Indian action films. With its gripping screenplay, memorable dialogues, and spectacular climax, KGF Chapter 2 delivers an unforgettable cinematic experience for fans of action, drama, and mass entertainment.

1.Animal - 114.7 million & BOX PFFICE COLLECTION: ₹900+ crore 😯

Animal is an action drama that explores the emotional and complicated relationship between a son and his father. The story follows Ranvijay, whose deep desire for his father's love and approval gradually transforms him into a fearless and relentless individual. As family conflicts, betrayal, and violence escalate, he is forced to navigate a dangerous world where loyalty, revenge, and sacrifice become central themes. Directed by Sandeep Reddy Vanga, the film combines emotional depth with intense action, stylish cinematography, and powerful performances. The movie examines the consequences of obsession, family bonds, and personal transformation while delivering gripping action sequences and dramatic storytelling. With its memorable soundtrack, impactful dialogues, and emotionally charged moments, Animal offers a unique blend of psychological drama and action that leaves a lasting impression on viewers.
